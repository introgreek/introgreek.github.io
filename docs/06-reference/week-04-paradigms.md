## Verbs

### Present Active Endings (-ω)

| Person | Singular | Plural |
| ----- | ----- | ----- |
| 1st | `-ω` | `-ομεν` |
| 2nd | `-εις` | `-ετε`|
| 3rd | **-ει** | **-ουσι(ν)** |

### Present Active Endings (-μι)

| Person | Singular | Plural |
| ----- | ----- | ----- |
| 1st | `-μι` | `-μεν` |
| 2nd | `-ς` | `-τε`|
| 3rd | **-σι(ν)** | **-ασι(ν)** |

### Sample Present Active Conjugated Verbs
